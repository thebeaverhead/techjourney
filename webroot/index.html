Technology Stack Overview

An enormous number of new software technologies come to see the light, it is easy to confuse their names with pokemons.

We've collected most of modern technologies abd grouped them into layers. These layers represent the level of stack these technologies belongs to and visually locate them as a dependecy of other layers top or down related.

If you feel we've missed something, have a look at git of this project. Feel free to report new technology as an issue or contribute with a pull request.

You also may wish to leave your email here, well let you know once we do a significant update of the technology stack.
